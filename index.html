  • Improves flexibility and decreases stiffness
  • Promotes full body recovery and balance
  • Supports full-body movement coordination
  • Helps in regaining mobility post-injury or post-op

  • Supports the body's natural healing abilities

  • Reduces physical and mental stress through nervous system regulation
