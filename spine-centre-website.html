Expert Treatments

What We Specialize In

Our team of certified physiotherapists provide evidence-based treatments across a wide spectrum of conditions.

🦴

Spine Physiotherapy

Back pain, neck pain, sciatica, slip disc (PIVD), spinal stenosis, ankylosing spondylitis, and cervical spondylosis.

Speciality
🏃

Sports Rehabilitation

ACL/PCL injuries, ankle sprains, tennis elbow, rotator cuff, hamstring strains, and full return-to-sport programs.

⚡

Electrotherapy

TENS, IFT, ultrasound therapy, shortwave diathermy, laser therapy, and NMES for pain relief and healing.

🧘

Posture Correction

Ergonomic assessment, postural re-education, and targeted exercises for desk workers, students, and drivers.

🧠

Neurological Rehab

Stroke recovery, Parkinson's, nerve injuries, Bell's palsy, and balance & coordination disorders.

🏥

Post-Surgical Rehab

Structured recovery after spine surgery, knee/hip replacement, or any orthopedic procedure.

👴

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Specialized care for elderly patients including fall prevention, arthritis management, and mobility improvement.

💉

Dry Needling / Acupuncture

Trigger point dry needling for myofascial pain, muscle knots, headaches, and chronic pain conditions.

🤸

Manual Therapy

Mobilization, manipulation, soft tissue therapy, myofascial release, and joint mobilization techniques.
